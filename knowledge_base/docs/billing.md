# Billing & Payments

## Refund policy
We offer a full refund within 14 days of the charge for monthly plans, and within 30 days for annual plans. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Customers on enterprise contracts must contact their account manager.

## Failed payments
If a charge fails we retry automatically after 24h, 72h, and 7 days. After three failed attempts the account is downgraded to the free tier. Customers can update their card under Settings → Billing.

## Pro-rated upgrades
Mid-cycle upgrades are pro-rated based on remaining days in the billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal — no partial credit is issued for downgrades.

## Invoices and receipts
Invoices are emailed automatically on the renewal date and are also available in Settings → Billing → Invoices. Tax IDs can be added per workspace.
