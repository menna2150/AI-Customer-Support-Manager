# Cancellation & Downgrades

## How to cancel
Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; the customer keeps full access until then.

## Data after cancellation
Workspace data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. During that window the customer can reactivate with a single click. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted in line with our retention policy.

## Win-back offers
Customers cancelling a paid plan are eligible for a 20% off coupon valid for 60 days. The coupon is offered automatically in the cancellation flow and cannot be stacked with other promotions.

## Enterprise contracts
Annual enterprise contracts are non-cancellable mid-term unless a material breach has occurred. Renewal opt-out must be submitted in writing 30 days before the renewal date.
